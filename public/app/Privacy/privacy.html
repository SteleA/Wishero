Instead of just copy pasting a template privacy policy text we have tried to write it so anyone can understand.

Password The password you have chosen for your account is stored in our database. It is strongly encrypted and secure. Don't use the same password everywhere!

Tracking We use tracking software to understand what users are doing on Wishero. Not in a creepy way. We want to gather user interaction data enough for us to understand and improve the service.

Cookies A cookie is a small text file stored on your computer. It contains various information about your interaction with web sites. We use cookies to identify your visits and how you interact with our service.

Newsletter When you sign up for Wishero you will be asked to confirm your account. When you are a confirmed user you will be added to a newsletter. If you wish to opt-out you may do so by clicking the unsubscribe link in the newsletter. We promise to send only useful information and not to spam you.
